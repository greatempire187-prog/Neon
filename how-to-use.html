🚀 Getting Started

1. Create Account

Sign up for your free Borderless NeoBank account. Choose your plan, verify your email, and set up your profile.

Steps:
  1. Visit signup page
  2. Fill in your personal and business information
  3. Choose your primary currency
  4. Accept terms and create account
  5. Access your dashboard immediately

2. Login to Dashboard

Access your personalized dashboard with real-time insights, account management, and financial tools.

Access Methods:
  • Regular Login: Use your email and password
  • Admin Access: Use admin credentials for advanced features
  • Quick Login: Use biometric or saved credentials
