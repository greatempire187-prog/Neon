Powerful Features

Everything You Need to Go Global

From multi-currency accounts to AI-powered bookkeeping, we've built the complete financial operating system for modern businesses.

Virtual Multi-Jurisdictional Accounts

Instantly issue local account numbers in the US (USD), UK (GBP), EU (EUR), Canada (CAD), and Nigeria (NGN) via BaaS partnerships.

Programmable Money (Agentic Rules)

Set "If/Then" commands for cash automation. Example: "If my USD balance hits $10k, sweep the surplus into my NGN account."

Corporate Virtual Cards

Issue cards with AI-enforced spend policies that block "out-of-policy" transactions at the point of sale.

Zero-Entry Bookkeeping

AI agents categorize 99% of bank transactions in real-time. Autonomous reconciliation matches statements to receipts automatically.

Multi-Standard Reporting

One-click toggle between GAAP (US), IFRS (Global), and local standards (FIRS/LIRS). OCR document vault for batch invoice processing.

CFO Mode (Strategic Brain)

Predictive treasury AI analyzes global interest rates and suggests moving idle cash into high-yield funds across currencies.

Debt Recovery Agent

Autonomous AI agents contact debtors via WhatsApp/Email, escalating the tone based on payment history.

Instant Global Settlements

Integrated stablecoin rails (USDC/PYUSD) for 24/7 cross-border B2B payments, bypassing SWIFT delays.

ESG & Carbon Audit

Automatically tracks company carbon footprint based on logistics and energy spending for 2026 sustainability compliance.

Global Coverage

Support for Major World Currencies

Hold, send, and receive in multiple currencies with instant conversion and competitive exchange rates.
